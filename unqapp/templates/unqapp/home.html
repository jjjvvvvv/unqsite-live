House Soft Wash

Why should I have my house soft washed?

You and your family’s health is a top priority, but the humid and wet Florida weather turns the exterior of your home into a breeding ground for mold and mildew. If the mold and mildew are not removed through the soft wash process, it starts to seep into your home through windows, vents, doors and many other small openings in the home.

Mold and mildew can have negative health effects such as; allergic reactions, sneezing, rashes, and if you have more severe mold allergies it can cause shortness of breath. If you have asthma, and you were to breathe in the mold spores it could trigger an asthma attack. The elderly, infants, and children are especially prone to being sensitive to mold spores in the air. Unconquered Services cares about the health of the community and doesn't want you or your loved ones to suffer through the unnecessary consequences of living with mold and mildew.

If you are looking into selling your home, power washing can add $10,000 - $15,000 to the sale price of the home, according to the National Association of Realtors.

We utilize the Soft Wash process to provide a deeper and safer clean to your home.

Low Pressure: We use very low pressure to apply a solution that lifts up the dirt and organic debris from the exterior of your home. We then rinse this away with clean water and low pressure. This process is superior to pressure washing as pressure washing only takes off the top layer of dirt, and forces water into the siding, windows, doors. This causes the mold to spread faster due to water being forced in places it can’t drain from. Without the high pressure, there’s no need to worry about your paint prematurely fading or peeling off.

Deep Clean: We use a 100% environmentally-friendly, biodegradable solution that eliminates all of the dirt and organic growth that breaks down the exterior of your home, and makes it look dirty. Instead of just washing it away with traditional pressure washing, we kill the mold spores so it is much more difficult for the mold to regrow, meaning a cleaner home for longer.

Concrete / Patio Wash

If you have dirty, slippery, and deteriorating concrete, we can bring it back to life

The porous nature of concrete allows dirt and moisture to seep into the concrete leaving discoloration and organic growth. Unconquered Services' two-step process will bring back that bright, clean, concrete back. We pull all of the dirt and discoloring elements out using commercial grade equipment. Then we treat it with a solution that seeps deep down into the pores, and kills off all of the organic growth, leaving you with beaming, beautiful concrete.
